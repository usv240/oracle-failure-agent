# The Failure Oracle — Demo Script v4 (Humanized cut)
**Told like a person, not a press release · ~3:25 at a natural pace · record the voiceover at 1.0×**

> **The story in one line:** Almost every startup that failed left a warning in its own numbers. Nobody read it in time. The Oracle reads it in seconds — argues both sides, shows the way out, and gets smarter every time.

> **What changed from v3:** the screens and beats are identical. The *spoken* lines were rewritten to sound like you talking to a friend — varied rhythm, natural phrasing, fewer of the perfectly-balanced "AI" pairs. It's a touch longer than the punchy cut (that's the cost of sounding human); trim levers are at the bottom if you need to claw back time.

---

## Golden rules (read before recording)
- **Record the voiceover at a natural, unhurried pace — like you're telling someone a story.** This is the master track; the silent screen recording gets trimmed to match your words.
- **Hold the three money shots — don't rush them:** the **Oracle Score hitting 0 / CRITICAL**, the **DISPUTED** badge, and the **Cascade graph**. Let each one breathe.
- **Read it out loud before you record.** If a line feels stiff in your mouth, change it to how *you'd* actually say it. The script is a starting point, not a cage.
- Dark mode on · browser zoom 90% (110% for the Oracle Score shot only).

## Pre-flight setup (do before you hit record)
- App open on the **Landing hero**, WeWork **not yet run** (we run it live).
- **Clear WeWork's run history** (Monthly Tracking → Clear) so there's no "previous context."
- **Pre-run the Decision Auditor + Pre-Mortem** (Act 14): Decision Auditor tab → **"Hire before PMF"** example → **Audit This Decision** → **"Run Pre-Mortem →"** and let it finish, so both are on screen to scroll.
- **Slack channel open** with a pre-existing alert visible (don't trigger live — just cut to it).
- **"How it Works → Tech"** panel reachable in one click.

# WHY JUDGES WILL LOVE IT

**It's a story, not a feature tour.** One spine — *the warning was in the numbers, nobody read it in time* — opens it (Act 1), pays off at the warning signals (*"five months, wasted"* — Act 8), and lands at the close. Every beat hands to the next.

**Anyone can follow it, but the rigor is right there to see.** The voice stays plain and human so a non-technical judge never gets lost; the exact figures — 54× vs 4×, 3% survival, 150 days — sit on screen where they prove this is real, not hand-waving.

**It maps cleanly to all three judging axes:**

| Judging axis | Where it shows up |
|---|---|
| **Google Cloud — ADK, Gemini, Vertex AI** | 3-agent pipeline (Act 3), live terminal, engine callout (Act 15), Cloud Run + Cloud Scheduler (Acts 13, 15) |
| **MongoDB — breadth + depth, in the critical path** | Vector + keyword search (Act 3), graph cascade (Act 10), self-learning change streams (Acts 10, 15), cohort analytics (Act 14), the full Atlas stack (engine, Act 15) |
| **Quality of idea** | The agent that **disagrees** (Act 6), the deterministic **escape plan** (Act 9), the **self-improving cascade** (Act 10) — three things most demos can't show |

**The moment a judge remembers:** Act 6 — a second agent that *pushes back on the first*. Most demos show an AI being confidently right. Yours shows it cross-examining itself.

## Timing math (honest numbers)
- ~555 words of narration. At a natural storyteller pace (~150 wpm) with the three held beats, that lands around **3:25–3:35**.

- **Fastest ways to claw back time if you need a hard ~3:05:**

- The verdict (Act 4) stays short on purpose — it's the climax. Don't pad it; let the silence carry it.
